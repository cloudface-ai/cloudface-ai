Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

Introduction

CloudFace AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our face recognition and photo processing services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address and Google account information
  • Profile information from Google authentication
  • Usage data and service preferences

Photo and Image Data

  • Photos uploaded from Google Drive
  • Face recognition data and embeddings
  • Processing results and metadata

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our face recognition services
  • Process and analyze uploaded photos
  • Generate face embeddings for recognition
  • Improve our AI algorithms and services
  • Communicate with you about service updates

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Photos are processed locally and temporarily stored
  • Face embeddings are stored securely for recognition purposes
  • We implement strict access controls and security measures

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • For service providers who assist in our operations

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data processing
  • Control your privacy settings
